# Shared World Chapter 5 - v0.34

## Scope

`Dawa Ka Sach` now runs at the connected Seva Hospital campus inside `FreeRoam`. Chapter 4 can advance directly into the hospital morning without reloading Prayagraj, while retaining the same player, camera, minimap, save, vehicles, ambience, and compact mission HUD.

## Playable Flow

1. Bring Shanti to the emergency bay and keep a receipt-backed transport record.
2. Share her history with triage, read the consent, and activate verified blood donors.
3. Inspect the pharmacy stock register and preserve the mismatched medicine batch label.
4. Receive the doctor's private, non-graphic update that Shanti is stable but the pregnancy was lost.
5. Choose a verified case file or a mechanically weaker instant public post.
6. Obtain a privacy-safe certified tender copy and hand sealed evidence to Samrat.
7. Meet Shanti in the recovery garden and commit to evidence-led system reform.

The verified route contains twelve ordered objectives and ends at Trust 97, Funds Rs 150, Reputation 71, and Proof 17.

## Safety And Presentation

- Existing Seva Hospital landmark with a walkable emergency canopy, ambulance lane, pharmacy stock booth, certified records desk, and recovery garden
- No graphic medical scene, real hospital allegation, real supplier, real political party, or public disclosure of private records
- Shanti survives and remains an active partner; the family loss is handled through dialogue rather than visual spectacle
- The verified route earns stronger Proof and Reputation than the instant-post route
- The minimap hides during the strategy modal and hospital morning settings restore automatically after the mission
