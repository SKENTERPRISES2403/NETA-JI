# Chapter 24: Seva Se Vishwa Guru

## Purpose

Chapter 24 completes Prototype 1 with a fictional global-cooperation review. India does not capture a world map or dominate real countries. Leadership is earned through transparent trade, open science, defensive peace, humanitarian response, climate action, and accountable public institutions.

## Global Leadership State

- `TRADE`: fair contracts, standards, worker safety, payment reliability, and dispute appeals
- `SCIENCE`: open research, scholarships, rural labs, safety review, reproducibility, and accessible technology
- `PEACE`: defensive readiness, civilian oversight, cyber safety, rescue logistics, and veterans care
- `HUMANITY`: humanitarian relief, climate resilience, local credit, consent, and grievance access
- `SCORE`: weighted independent global leadership review
- `VISHWA GURU`: earned only when national development passed, every global pillar is at least 75, and the final score is at least 88
- Save v24 stores all four pillars, the strategy decision, review score, and outcome

## Review Calculation

The score combines Trade Trust (22%), Science and Innovation (23%), Peace and Defensive Readiness (20%), Humanitarian and Climate Leadership (23%), National Development (8%), Institutional Trust (4%), Reputation (3%), and a Pressure penalty (4%).

The title is deliberately conditional. A highly visible route can complete the story but cannot earn the outcome when defensive restraint or another pillar falls below the independent gate.

## Playable Work

- Public global-service charter with privacy, correction, appeal, and audit rules
- Fair-trade corridor for small exporters, farmers, workers, and consumers
- Open-science mission with rural labs, scholarships, health technology, and reproducible evidence
- Defensive peace, cyber safety, civilian oversight, rescue, and disaster-readiness drill
- Humanitarian and climate network with need-based aid and complaint access
- Binding Seva + Saath Compact versus Superpower Spectacle decision
- Open technology, accessibility, bias, procurement, and interoperability audit
- Climate-response coalition covering heat, rivers, energy, crops, finance, and rescue
- Two-way knowledge and culture exchange with translation and local-language access
- Shanti-led citizen accountability review
- Samrat's local-to-global ground-truth audit, including weak cases
- Independent computed four-pillar review and final Prototype 1 ceremony

## Safety Rules

- Every country-facing programme, partner, official, result, dispute, and allegation is fictional
- No real foreign government, political leader, party, war, border claim, or election result is represented
- Military power is framed as defensive deterrence, civilian oversight, rescue, and disaster support
- Humanitarian aid is need-based and never carries party branding
- Vishwa Guru is an earned cooperation outcome, not a claim of cultural or military superiority
- Shanti and Samrat remain independent accountability voices rather than political mascots

## Verified Outcomes

- Seva + Saath Compact: Power 89, Team 750, Pressure 72, Trade 92, Science 100, Peace 94, Humanity 100, Score 96, Vishwa Guru earned
- Superpower Spectacle: Power 93, Team 744, Pressure 83, Trade 100, Science 84, Peace 74, Humanity 88, Score 87, Vishwa Guru not earned
- Both routes persist decision choices 1 and 2, complete all twelve objectives, and preserve the passed national-development foundation
