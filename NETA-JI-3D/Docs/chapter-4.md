# Chapter 4: Operation Umeed

## Purpose

Chapter 4 resolves Sandhya's abduction through a police-led, non-graphic rescue. It introduces the first reusable player decision and makes safety, patience, money, Trust, and Reputation produce different persisted outcomes.

## Objective Flow

1. Take the perimeter brief from Constable Samrat.
2. Verify the suspect van against the CCTV evidence.
3. Lock Sandhya's short-range watch signal.
4. Choose a coordinated entry or risky solo side-gate approach.
5. Unlock the backup gate and transmit the patrol signal.
6. Reach Sandhya and provide basic first aid.
7. Secure the fictional gang ledger without contaminating evidence.
8. Complete police evidence handover.
9. Reunite Sandhya with Shanti at the family safe point.

## Decision Contract

- `Samrat Ka Plan`: costs Rs 100, grants Trust +3 and Reputation +3.
- `Akela Side Gate`: costs no funds, loses Trust 4 and Reputation 3.
- The selected approach persists in save v4 as `rescueApproach`.
- Movement is locked while the decision panel is open.
- The same decision interface can be reused for elections, policy, opposition, and governance chapters.

## Safety Contract

- Sandhya is found conscious and is never shown being harmed.
- No playable combat, revenge, torture, or vigilante punishment occurs.
- Police backup, medical support, evidence handling, and family safety lead the mission.
- The gang, case, evidence, and locations are fictional.
