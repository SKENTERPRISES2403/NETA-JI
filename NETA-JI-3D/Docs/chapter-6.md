# Chapter 6: India Helping Party

## Purpose

Chapter 6 begins Azad's political journey. Community service becomes an accountable fictional organization with separate funds, a written constitution, founding members, a public charter, and a local ward-election path.

## Political Organization Mechanics

- `POWER`: organizational ability to contest, negotiate, and execute.
- `TEAM`: verified volunteers and founding workers.
- `PRESSURE`: opposition attention, conflict, and campaign risk.
- These persist with Trust, Funds, Reputation, and Proof in save v6.

## Founding Safeguards

- Helpers Hand remains nonpartisan; NGO staff, office, donor data, and money stay separate.
- Constable Samrat remains a neutral police officer and does not join the party.
- India Helping Party, all members, rivals, filings, and allegations are fictional.
- The temporary symbol is Helping Hands, not the Indian national flag or a real party symbol.
- Founding rules ban hate speech and require internal elections, audited accounts, disclosures, and grievance records.
