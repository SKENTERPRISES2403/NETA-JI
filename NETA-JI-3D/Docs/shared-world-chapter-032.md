# Shared World Chapter 3 - v0.32

## Scope

`Sandhya Kahan Hai?` now runs in the connected Prayagraj `FreeRoam` scene. It follows Chapter 2 without reloading the city and uses the same player, camera, minimap, save state, vehicles, ambience, and compact mission HUD.

## Playable Flow

1. Meet Shanti at the Allahpur home and confirm Sandhya is missing.
2. Inspect Sandhya's dropped ribbon and record Meera's last-seen account.
3. Restore the fictional Gupta Chai CCTV backup and verify a partial van plate and route.
4. Activate privacy-safe Helpers Hand search teams without public rumours.
5. Give the evidence to Constable Samrat at a neutral night help desk.
6. Verify the incident record and a police-led, no-solo-entry search route.
7. Return home and record the fictional extortion call with police support.

The mission contains eleven ordered objectives and ends at Trust 67, Funds Rs 900, and Reputation 40.

## Safety And Presentation

- Deep-blue temporary night atmosphere with warm search pools and neutral police beacons
- Allahpur home staging, a fictional CCTV tea stall, volunteers with torches, search desk, police desk, evidence board, and route map
- No combat, graphic harm, vigilantism, real allegation, real gang, or real political party
- Evidence is shared only through verified teams and neutral police channels
- The actual non-graphic police-led rescue remains Chapter 4
- Night settings restore automatically when the mission ends
