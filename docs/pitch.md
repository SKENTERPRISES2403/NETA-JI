# NETA JI Pitch Notes

## Product Summary

NETA JI is a fictional, mobile-first comedy election arcade game. Players create a safe fictional party, choose a color and symbol, run yatra-style routes, close loops to win booths, and unlock meme-ready result posters.

## Why It Can Sell

- Instant hook: party name, color, symbol, and state choice create personal attachment.
- Viral loop: funny fictional events, supporter crowds, and shareable result posters are reel-friendly.
- Low friction: static offline PWA, installable from mobile browser, no backend dependency for demo.
- Safety-first theme: real parties, leaders, caste, religion, hate, violence, and abuse terms are blocked locally.
- Credible map: real state/UT silhouettes from open-license ADM1 data, with visible attribution and a mobile-safe point budget.

## Target Audience

- Mobile casual players who like quick territory games.
- Indian meme/reel audiences who respond to fictional political comedy.
- Publishers or studios looking for a lightweight viral prototype.

## Demo Script

1. Open the PWA link on a phone.
2. Tap `Quick Demo`, or open with `?demo=1`.
3. Press `OK` on Uttar Pradesh to enter the pitch arena.
4. Swipe to run the yatra and close a loop.
5. Show that a small loop wins only its enclosed booths, then cut a rival route to convert its supporters.
6. Show events, a multi-loop mandate win, and the result poster export.
7. Open `Pitch` from the side panel to show safety and product positioning.

## Reviewer Checklist

- Fresh setup works with custom fictional party.
- `Quick Demo` preloads the safe preset and highlighted UP arena.
- `?demo=1` opens the same pitch-ready flow.
- Mobile swipe movement feels responsive.
- Loop close gives visual, sound, haptic, and feed feedback.
- Small loops never fill the whole state and do not cause an instant win.
- Opponent conversion shows supporters joining the rally.
- Mandate result displays victory ceremony and poster/share controls.
- Install button remains available when the browser supports PWA install.
- Reset clears local progress.
- Blocked real politics and sensitive identity terms are rejected.

## Monetization Options

- Rewarded cosmetics for party banners, outfits, and poster styles.
- Premium meme poster templates.
- Seasonal fictional campaign packs.
- Non-political local event skins using the same territory arcade engine.

## Production Notes

The current pitch build is offline-only. Map geometry is CC-licensed and attributed; Survey of India material is reference-only because its current reproduction policy requires written permission. Before public launch, add server-side moderation, report/review tools for shared names/posters, store-safe privacy copy, real-device QA, and a final legal review.
